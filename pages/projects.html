__ Research Projects & Tools _____________________

ADEMA – Automated Data Efficient Morphometric Approach

Affiliation: IIT Roorkee (2024–2026)

A global framework for classifying lentic and lotic inland waters down to 0.09 hectares. Developed to improve large-scale inland water inventory, classification and hydrological assessment.

Python Machine Learning Earth Observation GIS

StoRI – Storage and Reachability Index

Affiliation: IIT Roorkee (2025–2026)

Novel watershed-scale index developed to quantify trade-offs between water storage and water reachability, providing a more realistic understanding of water availability.

Hydrology Remote Sensing Python Water Resources

TOI – Tankshed Overflow Index

Affiliation: IIT Roorkee (2024–2026)

Novel framework designed to identify suitable locations for new waterbodies within watersheds and evaluate basin-scale flood mitigation potential.

Hydrological Modelling Flood Analysis GIS Remote Sensing

C-DAS – Cascaded Depth and Storage Models

Affiliation: IIT Roorkee (2025–2026)

Machine learning and deep learning framework for estimating bathymetric characteristics, mean depth and storage volume of global inland waters.

Deep Learning Machine Learning Python Earth Observation

IndiaRice500

Affiliation: IIT Roorkee (2024–2026)

Long-term gridded rice yield dataset for India generated using self-supervised digital twins integrating Earth Observation, climate and agricultural management datasets.

AI Satellite Data Agriculture Python

CropLizer

Affiliation: IIT Roorkee (2025–2026)

Farm-specific digital advisory platform supporting fertilizer and water management decisions for smallholder rice farmers.
